Introduction

Established in 2005 and duly registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Surveying and Valuation (ESVRBON) as well as the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja, Peter Abiade & Co has built a strong reputation for integrity, quality, and professionalism. Our firm is founded on principles of transparency and excellence, ensuring every project is completed with the highest standards.

With extensive expertise accumulated over more than a decade even before our formal establishment, our dedicated team is proficient in property valuation, comprehensive market analysis, and meticulous property management. We provide a full range of estate agency services that cover residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational properties. Our goal is to deliver exceptional value while safeguarding our clients' interests at every stage.

By adopting an off-plan approach, we engage our clients right from the architectural design phase through to construction and final completion. This transparent, step-by-step process ensures that your vision is realized accurately and with care, fostering long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual success.

Headquartered at Office 1 Mistkay Plaza, 551 Agege Motor Road, Ladipo-Shogunle, Lagos, our commitment to sustainable practices and innovative solutions has enabled us to grow a diverse management portfolio. At Peter Abiade & Co, we are dedicated to creating developments that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also environmentally responsible and economically viable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize real estate development by integrating sustainable practices with innovative design. We are committed to delivering projects of exceptional quality while actively involving our clients at every stage—from conceptual design to construction and completion. Through transparency, integrity, and a customer-focused approach, we create vibrant communities that stand as a testament to our dedication and expertise.

Innovation

Peter Abiade & Co is at the forefront of real estate innovation, seamlessly merging advanced digital technologies with sustainable design. By harnessing the power of AI, IoT, and data-driven insights, we optimize every aspect of our projects—from smart construction techniques to dynamic property management—ensuring efficiency, resilience, and an unparalleled living experience. Our visionary approach not only creates eco-friendly and technologically integrated communities but also sets new industry standards for modern, adaptive, and inspirational developments.
