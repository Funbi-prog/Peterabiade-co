Who We Are

PETER ABIADE & CO. is a professional firm of estate surveyors & valuers, established in 2005 and fully registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Surveying and Valuation (ESVRBON) throughout the Federation, as well as with the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja. Our team of seasoned experts excels in property valuation, market analysis, and comprehensive property management.

We provide a full spectrum of estate agency services—from negotiating leases and sales to procuring residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational properties. In addition, our rigorous feasibility and viability appraisals, along with specialized real estate consultancy, set us apart.

While our credentials empower us to practice every facet of estate surveying and valuation, our principal focus remains property management, driven by leadership with over a decade of industry expertise. With unwavering confidence and commitment, we deliver excellence that inspires trust in every project.
